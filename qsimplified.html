QSimplified

Designed for high-impact environments like banks, hospitals, and service centers, QSimplified combines real-time queuing, digital signage, and analytics to ensure every customer is served efficiently and professionally.

What is QSimplified?

QSimplified is OcTech’s digital queue management solution that improves customer flow, reduces wait times, and enhances the service experience. It integrates seamlessly with ticket printers, digital signage, and real-time dashboards.

Core Features

  • Touchscreen ticket kiosks
  • Content Management multimedia System
  • Staff dashboard & branch control
  • SMS notifications
  • Daily reports & performance analytics
  • QR code-based Feedback system

Why Choose QSimplified?

Built with simplicity in mind, QSimplified is easy to deploy, cost-effective, and customizable to your service environment — trusted by leading institutions for delivering faster service, sharper insights, and a consistently better customer experience.
